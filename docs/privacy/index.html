Privacy Policy

Effective date: 26 September 2024

Your privacy is important to us. Our app helps you track orders and manage contacts and orders.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Authentication is handled by Firebase. No sensitive user information is stored on our servers.

2. Firebase

We use Firebase as our backend service. Firebase authentication and database services are used to handle login and store order data. Firebase may collect some information according to their own Privacy Policy.

3. Contact and Order Management

Our app allows you to manage your contacts and track orders. This data is securely stored in Firebase Firestore, and no additional user information is collected or shared.

4. Data Retention

Your order and contact data will be stored until you delete your account or uninstall the app.

5. Security

We implement security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
